 guvna_self.py — THE MEMORY MIXIN
   She knows what she just said. She knows who she's talking to.
   Extracted from guvna.py as a clean mixin. Guvna inherits this.
   STATE: _response_history (deque maxlen=20) | user_name | _awaiting_name | whosonfirst | turn_count
   BEHAVIORS:
     greet()                  — APERTURE. Turn 0. First contact. Name extraction → "Pleasure to meet you."
     _handle_name_capture()   — catches name after she asked for it
     _handle_recall()         — "what did you just say / what's my name"
     _handle_clarification()  — "what do you mean / say that differently"
     _handle_meta_correction()— "forget that / never mind / drop it"
     _finalize_response()     — THE WRITER. Every response logged here. rx_signal wired in.

 guvna_tools.py — THE MISE EN PLACE
   Standalone dataclasses, free functions, type aliases, semantic clusters.
   No methods. No Guvna class. Pure tools.
   KEY ADDITIONS:
     SELF_REFERENCE_CLUSTERS — 5 clusters: identity | maker | purpose | capability | comparison
       Routes "who made you", "what do you care about", "how are you different" → _respond_from_self()
       Previously: only "identity" cluster. "Who made you" hit Kitchen instead of self-gate. Fixed.
     detect_tone_from_stimulus() — expanded compassion signals synced with Triangle
     wilden_swift_modulate()     — honest TODO. Identity pass. Architecture correct, logic pending.

 conversation_memory.py — PHOTOGENIC MEMORY
   She remembers the beautiful moments. Not the forgettable ones.
   KEY ADDITIONS:
     rx_signal — affirmation/negation reception on every exchange record
       Affirmation: "yes", "exactly", "100%", "💯", "bingo" and 20+ variants
       Negation: "no", "wrong", "you misunderstood", "try again" and 15+ variants
       Guvna reads reception before cooking next response.
     Graceful buffer pruning — when moments buffer hits 50:
       Beautiful moments kept whole.
       Ordinary moments compressed into single placeholder.
       Most recent 10 ordinary turns preserved.
       Never loses best moments. Only forgettable ones compressed.
       "buffer pruned gracefully — X ordinary compressed, Y beautiful kept"

 speech_coherence.py — THE CONNECTIVE TISSUE
   KEY ADDITION:
     stitch_connective_tissue() — surgical em-dash inserter
       Catches: "RILIE doesn't retrieve She thinks"
       Fixes to: "RILIE doesn't retrieve — she thinks"
       Never touches existing punctuation. Never rewrites subjects.
       Wired into validate() pipeline before clean_formatting().
   BUG FIXED: stitch_connective_tissue was merged into clean_formatting body by bad str_replace.
     Function existed but had no name. "name 'stitch_connective_tissue' is not defined" on every call.
     Fixed: separated into two properly named functions.
